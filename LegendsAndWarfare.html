Legends and Warfare
Minion Behaviors
As a core gameplay mechanic of a MOBA, how minions behave is extremely important. Using Unity NavMesh, I implemented pathing and navigation throughout the map. Furthermore, the minions come in two types, melee (RoboDogs), and ranged (EnforcerBots) each with their own attack types and targeting systems. On top of these AI mechanics,I also worked on networking the minions over several machines for multiplayer continuity, as well as implemented animations and sounds created by the other members of the team.
Tower Targeting
Towers within Legends and Warfare exist in order to provide obstacles to the players as well as divide the map and create exciting gameplay. I worked on implementing the targeting and firing for the towers, and making them interact correctly between players and other minions over a multiplayer network.
